Our 10 Commitments to Safety

Safety is not an after-thought; it is the core foundation of our engineering practice. Here are the ten primary safety protocols we implement on every project:

  1. Structural Load Verification: We perform exhaustive load calculations for all slabs, columns, and pre-cast concrete elements to ensure they exceed minimum Indian Standards (IS) codes.
  2. Site-Specific Design Audits: We conduct detailed soil testing and seismic audits to customize the structural design to the specific environmental context of the plot.
  3. Strict QC/QA Standards: Quality Control and Quality Assurance protocols are implemented at every stage of construction, from excavation to finishing.
  4. Material Certification: We use only IS-certified and tested materials (steel, concrete mix design, bricks) to ensure long-term durability and safety.
  5. On-Site Supervision: Syed Amaan Hussain provides personal, on-site supervision to ensure that the AutoCAD structural plans are executed with absolute precision by the construction team.
  6. Error-Free Visual Planning: By visualizing your project through complex 2D CAD schematics, we identify and resolve structural conflicts early, preventing costly and unsafe last-minute field adjustments.
  7. Transitioning Global Safety Codes: Leveraging international project experience through Ansari Consultancy, we transition high-tier international safety standards to local sites in India and The Gulf.
  8. Detailed Material Estimation: Accurate estimations of concrete and steel placement ensure the structure is built exactly as designed, with zero compromises in reinforcement.
  9. Cinematic Pre-Visualization: Our 3K AI Renders and Cinematic Walkthroughs allow us to pre-visualize and optimize the design for safety and aesthetics, ensuring structural integrity is maintained even in complex visual designs.
  10. Ansari Consultancy Integrity: As a powered-by division of Ansari Consultancy, we adhere to a rigid code of ethical engineering practice, prioritizing safety above all project timelines or costs.
